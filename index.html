I am a tenured Associate Professor in the Department of Management Science and Engineering at the School of Economics and Management, Tongji University.

My research lies at the intersection of data analytics, optimization, and transportation, with a particular emphasis on aviation operations. I develop data-driven optimization methods to address real-world challenges in airline planning, disruption management, and large-scale decision-making.
