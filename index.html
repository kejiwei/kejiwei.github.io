I am an Assistant Professor in Operations at the School of Economics and Management , Tongji University University.

My research focuses on the intersection of data analytics, optimization, and transportation, with a particular emphasis on the aviation industry. By leveraging data-driven approaches, I seek to address challenges in aviation operations and contribute to the optimization of transportation systems.
